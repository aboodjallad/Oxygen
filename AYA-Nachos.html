Chicken Nachos

These baked chicken nachos are cheesy, loaded with toppings, and irresistibly crunchy. Learn how to make our loaded chicken nachos in under 30 minutes!

Prep: 15 minutesCook: 10 minutesTotal: 25 minutes
Fat 17g Carbs 28g Protein 24g

Ingredients

    Meat
  • 1tablespoon olive oil
  • 1lb. ground chicken
  • 1packet taco seasoning (Or, sub 3 tablespoons homemade taco seasoning)
  • 3tablespoons water
    • Nachos + Toppings
  • 6 servings tortilla chips (of choice)
  • 1 large red pepper, diced
  • 1/2 medium red onion, diced
  • 1 can beans (black or pinto), drained and rinsed
  • 1/2 cup sweet corn
  • 1/2 cup Mexican shredded cheese (or more)
    • Optional Toppings
  • Greek yogurt
  • lime juice
  • fresh cilantro
  • slice avocado

Instructions

  1. First, prepare your ground chicken by heating 1 tablespoon of olive oil over medium/high heat in a large skillet.
  2. Then, add ground chicken and saute until fully cooked. Add taco seasoning and water and let simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Prepare nachos by lining a baking sheet with tin foil and preheating oven to 400ºF.
  4. Then add half of the chips and sprinkle on 1/2 of the toppings including the cooked meat. Then, do one more layer of chips and one more layer of toppings..
  5. Place baking sheet in the oven at 400ºF for 10 minutes or until cheese fully melts. Then, broil for 1-2 minutes.
  6. Serve with your favorite optional toppings such as Greek yogurt, lime juice, avocado, and fresh cilantro.

Tips & Notes

  • Everything Bagel Seasoning: we have not tested this with store-bought Everything Bagel Seasoning, but did get feedback it is much too salty. So, please make our homemade Everything Bagel Seasoning if you’re going to make these!
  • Nutrition Information excludes the greek yogurt dip.
